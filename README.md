# nuclear_PWNed

Start the game in a neighborhood where waves of mutated mobs are trying to kill you. You must first start with the boxing glove weapons, as that is all you could find. As you progress the waves on each level you will first unlock a baseball bat and then a pipe for use on the final waves of each level. You fight through arrow keys and action keys killing enemies and collecting supplies. Using certain rewards like vial of goop, it can be used to upgrade your stats. The red vial heals while the green vial increases speed. After defeating two waves, you are able to fight the boss where if killed you are rewarded with more “mutations”. Then in the next section another two waves will occur fighting new enemies before a final boss arrives. When you kill this final boss you beat the game. 

Background lore:
There was a nuclear war and people were advised to stay in their homes. The main character wakes up only to find that their mother is not home despite the warnings. They step out of their house and find a note from their mother. Upon finding out their mother left home, the main character sets off to find their mother in this new chaotic environment. 

After going to the office to find the mother, she finds a surgeon. This surgeon is the final boss and has been mutating all of the environment and surgically putting together monstrosities that the character had to fight. 

Controls:
Arrow keys to move, Z to attack, Enter/space to move to next wave
